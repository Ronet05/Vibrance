One shot
 11 AM

"This is an offline event where students will be presented with the five setups, each depicting the various aspects of photography. The themes given to the contestants include: Depth Contrast Exposure Object- Architecture Object- Model The contestants are presented with the setups on the day of the competition. They are given 15 minutes to take a single shot, which depicts the theme of the setup best. They are then given a few hours before the final submission to edit their shot. The final shot is then submitted which will be carefully curated by the judges and each photograph is given points according to the set criteria, the winner is declared based on the cumulative of the scores. Perspective
 11 AM

"Perspective is an offline event where the participants will be tested on the out-of-the-box observation skills. A set of objects will be placed in a room randomly. The objects can range from an ordinary pen to a piece of art. Some of the objects will be arranged in a more attractive manner while some haphazardly. The participants will then be given 5-10 minutes in the room each. They can click as many pictures as they like but only one can be submitted as an entry. The entries will then be examined by a panel of judges/voted upon through an online form and thus the winner will be declared."
